Properties

float Mass [get, set]
 Gets or sets the mass of the bone. High mass bones resist motion more than those of small mass. Setting the mass updates the inertia tensor of the bone.
 
ReadOnlyList< IKJoint > Joints [get]
 Gets the list of joints affecting this bone.
 
bool Pinned [get, set]
 Gets or sets whether or not this bone is pinned. Pinned bones cannot be moved by constraints.
 
bool IsActive [get, set]
 Gets whether or not the bone is a member of the active set as determined by the last IK solver execution.
 
float Radius [get, set]
 Gets or sets the radius of the bone. Setting the radius changes the inertia tensor of the bone.
 
float HalfHeight [get, set]
 Gets or sets the height, divided by two, of the bone. The half height extends both ways from the center position of the bone. Setting the half height changes the inertia tensor of the bone.
