enum BEPUphysics.PositionUpdating.PositionUpdateMode : byte

Update modes for position updateables. /summary>

Enumerator
Discrete 

Updates position discretely regardless of its collision pairs.

Passive 

Updates position discretely in isolation; when a Continuous object collides with it, its position update will be bounded by the time of impact.

Continuous 

Updates position continuously. Continuous objects will integrate up to their earliest collision time.
