Parameters
localSurfaceVerticesList of vertex positions on the surface of the convex hull shape, centered on the desired origin. These vertices are used as-is for the shape representation; no additional processing occurs.
descriptionCached information about the shape. Assumed to be correct; no extra processing or validation is performed.
